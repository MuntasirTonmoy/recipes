BEEF MEATLOAF

Ground beef mixed with onion, Panko breadcrumbs, fresh parsley and seasonings produces an irresistibly tender and juicy meatloaf. The sweet and tangy glaze adds amazing flavor to this classic meatloaf.

Prep time

15 min.

Cook time

1 hour

.

Servings

8

Tags: Beef

Instructions

Step 1

Mix the loaf ingredients, then transfer to a loaf pan.

Step 2

Mix the sauce ingredients, then pour over the loaf.

Step 3

Bake in the preheated oven until the meatloaf is no longer pink in the center.

Ingredients

1 ½ pounds ground beef

1 egg

1 onion, chopped

1 cup milk

1 cup dried bread crumbs

salt and pepper to taste

⅓ cup ketchup

2 tablespoons brown sugar

2 tablespoons prepared mustard

Tools

Baking Sheet

Frying Pan

Mixing Bowls

Measuring Cups
